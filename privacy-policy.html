Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 7, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Subject ("Game," "we," "us," "our," or "the Company"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience with our Android game. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information, including authentication data, game progress, and usage information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information from users of our Game:

  • Authentication Information: When you sign in using Google or Solana wallet, we collect your authentication credentials and profile information necessary to verify your identity.
  • Game Progress Data: We collect and store information about your gameplay, including:
    • Game score and achievements
    • Level progress and statistics
    • Game settings and preferences
    • Gameplay history and timestamps
  • Transaction Data: When you make in-app purchases or use in-app currency, we collect transaction records and payment information.
  • Device Information: We automatically collect information about your device, including device model, operating system, device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect data about how you interact with the Game, including features used, actions taken, and time spent playing.
  • Advertising Data: We may collect data related to advertisements shown to you, including ad impressions and ad interactions for optimization purposes.
  • Crash Reports: We may collect crash logs and diagnostic information to improve Game stability and performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate users and secure your account
  • To save and sync your game progress across devices
  • To provide and maintain in-app purchases and transactions
  • To display personalized advertisements and improve ad targeting
  • To improve and optimize the Game's functionality and user experience
  • To analyze usage patterns and trends to enhance our services
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues and maintain security
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights
  • To communicate with you about updates, new features, or important notices

3. Authentication Services

The Game supports authentication through the following services:

  • Google Authentication: When you sign in with your Google account, Google's privacy policies apply to the data Google collects. We receive your Google account information necessary for authentication.
  • Solana Wallet: When you authenticate using a Solana wallet, the blockchain wallet provider's privacy policies apply. We store the public wallet address for game progress synchronization.

You can manage your authentication preferences in your game settings at any time.

4. In-App Purchases and Payments

When you make in-app purchases:

  • Payment information is processed through Google Play's secure payment systems
  • We do not directly store your credit card or payment method information
  • Purchase history is recorded to track your transactions and validate entitlements
  • Transaction data is retained for billing, tax, and fraud prevention purposes

5. Advertising

Subject includes advertising to support the development and maintenance of the Game:

  • Ads are served through Google AdMob and may include personalized advertisements
  • We collect limited data about ad interactions to measure ad performance
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's advertising settings
  • Ad networks may collect their own data subject to their privacy policies

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Security measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit using industry-standard protocols
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the Game (analytics, hosting, payment processing, ad networks)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court orders, or government requests
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or sale of assets
  • Your Explicit Consent: When you authorize data sharing

8. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Game Progress Data: Retained as long as your account is active, and for 90 days after account deletion upon request
  • Transaction Data: Retained for 7 years for tax and legal compliance purposes
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data is retained indefinitely; individual analytics are retained for 12 months
  • Authentication Data: Retained as long as your account is active

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

Subject is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly and comply with COPPA requirements.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your personal information
  • The right to correct inaccurate information
  • The right to delete your information (right to be forgotten)
  • The right to restrict processing of your information
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to opt-out of personalized advertising

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
